The Method

The Tikrar Method — how scholars have memorized for 1,000 years

Tikrar isn't new. It's how every Hafiz in history preserved the Qur'an: review daily, review deeply, review strategically. At Safeenah, we don't just teach Tikrar — we enforce it. Every single class. Every single student.

Phase 01

New Lesson

Sabq Jadid

Your child memorizes 1 new page. Teacher listens, corrects, perfects.

⏱ 10–15 min
Phase 02

Recent Review

Sabq Qarib

Recite what was memorized last week (3–5 pages). Catches slips before they become permanent.

⏱ 10 min
Phase 03

Old Review

Sabq Qadeem

Recite from 1–2 months ago (20–30 pages). Builds long-term retention.

⏱ 15 min
Phase 04

Connection

Rabt

Link today's new lesson to yesterday's. Students learn continuity, not isolated verses.

⏱ 5–10 min
